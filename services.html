Trash Bin Cleaning Services in Savannah, GA

From single-family homes to commercial properties, we keep every type of bin clean, odor-free, and sanitized year-round.

The City of Savannah collects trash, but it doesn’t clean the containers. That job falls to homeowners and property managers — and in a climate as hot and humid as the Georgia coast, a neglected bin becomes a bacterial breeding ground within days of each pickup. Our hot-water mobile cleaning service covers residential curbside bins, commercial dumpster cans, and HOA common-area containers across Savannah and Chatham County.

Every service uses 200°F pressurized water and an enclosed capture system, so nothing drains into city stormwater. Contact us to schedule your first cleaning or ask about subscription plans.

Service 1

Residential Bin Cleaning

Our most popular service covers the standard two-bin household setup (one trash, one recycling). We schedule your cleaning on your existing Savannah pickup day so the bins are always empty when we arrive. No trip to the curb required on your part beyond what you already do each week.

Inside each cleaning cycle, your bin is lifted into the truck’s sealed hopper and exposed to a 360° rotating hot-water nozzle. The heat alone kills most household bacteria and dissolves stuck food residue. A biodegradable deodorizer finishes the job, leaving the bin smelling neutral rather than masking odors with artificial fragrance.

Savannah homeowners in neighborhoods like Ardsley Park, Midtown, and Southside find monthly service to be the sweet spot during summer, when 90°F+ temperatures make weekly trash cycles especially harsh on uncleaned bins. Quarterly service handles the shoulder seasons fine for most households.

Call us today for residential trash bin cleaning in Savannah.

Service 2

Commercial & HOA Bin Cleaning

Restaurants, apartment complexes, and HOAs face a different scale of bin contamination than single-family homes. A single restaurant dumpster can accumulate food grease, liquid waste, and bacteria colonies that produce persistent odors even between commercial pickups. HOA-managed bins that serve multiple units become saturated faster and carry health risks that affect the whole property.

We service commercial bins and dumpster carts on a schedule that aligns with your existing waste management contract. Properties in the historic district and along the Savannah waterfront have responded well to bi-weekly cleaning during the tourist season, when foot traffic and waste volumes spike.

Our enclosed capture system is fully compliant with local stormwater regulations — important for properties near the Savannah River or Forsyth Park where discharge rules are strictly enforced.

Call us today for commercial bin cleaning in Savannah, GA.

Service 3

One-Time & Move-In Bin Cleaning

Not every situation requires a recurring plan. New homeowners in Savannah who inherit bins from a previous occupant often find those containers in surprisingly bad shape — months or years of buildup that a standard hose-down at home can’t address. A single professional cleaning with 200°F water resets the bin to a sanitary baseline.

One-time cleanings are also popular after an extended Savannah summer heat wave, after a missed city pickup that left waste sitting an extra week, or as a seasonal reset heading into fall. There’s no subscription required and no minimum number of bins.

Call us today to book a one-time trash bin cleaning in Savannah.
