What We Do

What Is Trash Bin Cleaning in Savannah?

Trash bin cleaning is a professional sanitizing service that removes the bacteria, mold, and residue left behind after every trash pickup. A standard Savannah garbage truck empties your bin but leaves the grime inside — that organic residue is what causes odors, maggots, and health risks.

Our mobile cleaning unit comes to your curb on your scheduled day, lifts your bins into an enclosed wash hopper, and blasts every interior surface with 200°F pressurized water. The result is a bin that smells clean and carries a fraction of the bacteria it did before.

Homeowners in Savannah, Pooler, Richmond Hill, and Garden City use this service to control flies and odors, especially during the hot summer months when temperatures regularly push bins past the point of being tolerable.

When Do Savannah Residents Call Us?

You don’t have to live with a smelly bin. Most people reach out when one of these situations becomes impossible to ignore:

Summer Odors

From May through September, Savannah’s heat and humidity turn a slightly dirty bin into a neighborhood eyesore. Bacteria multiply rapidly above 85°F — that sweet-rotten smell means the problem is already established.

Flies & Maggots

A dirty bin is a fly breeding ground. If you’re seeing maggots inside your can, the residue has been there long enough to support a full lifecycle. One hot-water service clears the cycle and eliminates the conditions that attract flies.

After a Missed Pickup

When Savannah sanitation misses a collection, leftover waste sits an extra week in the heat. By the time the bin is emptied, the interior is significantly worse than usual — a professional cleaning resets it.

Moving Into a New Home

Inherited bins from a previous owner are often deeply contaminated. A new home in Savannah deserves a fresh start with bins that are actually clean, not just empty.
